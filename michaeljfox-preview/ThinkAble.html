Dear ,

As you prepare for your premiere party, we're sure that like us, you are thinking about what this party — and Michael's return to prime time — represents.

For us, this is about more than just a TV show. Michael believes we have to live today and reach for our goals. The same optimism and determination that led him to launch our Foundation have now brought him back to television. And they drive us forward every day on our path to a cure.

We know we're not alone. That's why we created think/able: a new project celebrating the power of optimism and determination to overcome our challenges and achieve our most audacious goals.

To help kick off this new project, will you write a message to Michael to let him know that you, too, believe in the power of optimism and action for reaching for our goals?

Here at the Foundation, we already think/able to power us and our community forward — the researchers working toward a cure in our lifetime, our grassroots community raising funds to push the most important science forward, PD patients and their friends and family. Every single one of us overcomes challenges every day by choosing to focus on what's possible.

With Michael and Parkinson's in the public eye on "The Michael J. Fox Show," we have the chance to welcome even more people who share this spirit into our community, to speed our path to a cure.

Michael has said: Whatever big dream is driving you, you only can't if you don't. For us, that means curing Parkinson's disease in our lifetime. But whatever challenges we face, we must face them with the same optimism and determination that Michael inspires.

Enjoy the party!

The Michael J. Fox Foundation for Parkinson's Research
