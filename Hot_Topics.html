Hot Topics Webinar:
The Long Road to Diagnosis

Thursday, January 16, 12 - 1 p.m. ET

Join The Michael J. Fox Foundation for our next Hot Topics Webinar, "The Long Road to Diagnosis" — where we'll talk to a patient about her process of and frustrations in obtaining a Parkinson's diagnosis and then hear from researchers on what avenues they're pursuing to shorten that journey.

MJFF Contributing Editor Dave Iverson will moderate a conversation with:

Linda Keilman
Parkinson's patient
Schererville, Indiana

Kathleen Shannon, MD
Professor, Department of Neurological Sciences
Rush Medical College
Senior Attending Physician
Rush University Medical Center

Mark Frasier, PhD
Vice President, Research Programs
The Michael J. Fox Foundation for Parkinson's Research

Q&A with participants will follow the discussion.

This Webinar will be the first in our 2014 "Third Thursdays" monthly series exploring the latest developments in Parkinson's disease research. Mark your calendar for each third Thursday at 12 p.m. ET to join us.
