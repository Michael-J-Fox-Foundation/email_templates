Your trial {{BriefTitle}} is waiting for you on Fox Trial Finder, a free online PD clinical trial recruitment tool with a database of health information for over 25,000 willing PD clinical trial volunteers (75% PD, 25% control). Created by The Michael J. Fox Foundation, Fox Trial Finder allows PD clinical researchers to review and connect with an organized group of patients and controls primed to participate in moving PD research forward.

TO GET STARTED:

  1. Register. Click here to create a trial team member account on Fox Trial Finder. (If you have already registered with Fox Trial Finder, skip this step and proceed to step 2.)
  2. Connect. Once you have created an account, click here to connect with your trial. You will be prompted to log in to the account you created in Step 1, and your trial will appear in your dashboard.
  3. Review Trial Details. You can make changes and save them in real-time should anything need to be updated. Assign coordinators to manage recruitment for individual site locations.
  4. View Volunteer Matches. Fox Trial Finder automatically identifies volunteer profiles of individuals that match your trial's eligibility criteria.
  5. Search the Volunteer Database. You have the ability to filter the 25,000+ volunteer profiles based on over 20 different characteristics.
  6. Send and Receive Messages. Found a volunteer that looks like a good fit? Send them a secure message on Fox Trial Finder to conduct further screening.

For more information about how Fox Trial Finder can support your recruitment efforts, check out the Fox Trial Finder Guide for Trial Teams.
