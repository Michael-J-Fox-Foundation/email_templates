Third Thursdays Webinar
How Stopping a Sticky Protein May Cure Parkinson's

Thursday, November 20, 12 to 1 p.m. ET

Alpha-synuclein is a "sticky" protein that clumps in brain cells of people with Parkinson's. Our panelists will discuss how these protein clumps may contribute to disease and how researchers are targeting alpha-synuclein to stop Parkinson's progression. Register today.

Q&A with participants will follow the discussion.
