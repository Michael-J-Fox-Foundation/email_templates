Dear Friends,

The Michael J Fox Foundation is seeking people with Parkinson's disease and caregivers for people with Parkinson's disease to give feedback on a set of interactive, web-based tools. These tools are designed to help those with Parkinson's manage their disease and donate data for Parkinson's research.

To this end, the Foundation is sponsoring a focus group at a location in downtown Boulder, Colorado, on Wednesday May 22nd. The focus group will last no more than two hours.

If selected, you will be asked to complete a series of tasks on a computer and give your honest opinion about the experience. There will be an open discussion, so you should be prepared to speak your mind. All information will be kept confidential.

If you are interested, please fill out this quick questionnaire and include your contact information. If you are selected to participate, a representative will reach out to you with additional details, including the specific time and address of the focus group.

Thank you for your interest in this this project.
